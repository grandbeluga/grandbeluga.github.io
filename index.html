Hey, I'm Ethan.

Welcome. I make ambient music and love sharing about that process, so I try to write about it weekly in my ambient focused newsletter betweenspace. I also write code, take pictures, and create all of my own branding; I love the process and opportunity to work with multiple art mediums in one project.

I'm not a huge fan of modern social media, so I wanted to have somewhere other than instagram to send people who ask about my music and whatever other hobby related things I make; if you're here, it's probably because of one of those conversations. I appreciate you.

Thanks for being here. To Jesus be the glory.
